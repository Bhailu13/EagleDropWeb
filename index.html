Delete Your Eagle Drop Account

If you wish to delete your Eagle Drop account and all associated personal data, please follow the steps below carefully:

  • Send an email to eagledrop.customer@gmail.com from the email address linked to your Eagle Drop account.
  • Include your registered phone number and the phrase "Delete Account" in the email subject.
  • We will process your request within 7 business days and notify you once the deletion is complete.

For more information on how we handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy.
