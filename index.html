Delete Your Eagle Drop Account

If you wish to delete your Eagle Drop account and associated personal data, please follow the steps below:

  • Send an email to eagledrop.customer@gmail.com from the email address linked to your account.
  • Include your registered phone number and the word "Delete Account" in the subject.
  • We will process your deletion request within 7 business days and confirm once complete.
